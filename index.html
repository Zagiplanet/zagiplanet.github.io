About ZAGI WORLD

After 10 years abroad, I returned to my homeland to create something meaningful – a family project full of love for nature, animals, and local tradition. ZAGI WORLD is a dream come true: a small farm, beautiful gardens, and a place for everyone to enjoy the countryside life at affordable prices.

Animals & Nature

We raise horses, goats, chickens, alpacas, rabbits and more – all with care and respect. We also grow traditional plants and trees, creating a natural oasis for learning and relaxation.

Trips & Activities

From educational visits for schools to fun family adventures – ZAGI WORLD offers affordable day trips, glamping, seasonal events and delicious homemade food. Come and experience real rural joy!
